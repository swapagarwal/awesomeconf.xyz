About The Conference

AwesomeConf is a not-for-profit conference that aims to cover the latest developments in the dev and design domains, by bringing a diverse set of people together. We strive to make conferences more engaging and leave you with wanting more. We keep experimenting to ensure that attending the event is a good use of your time!
